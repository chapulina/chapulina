Master's thesis

In 2013 I received a double master's degree from Warsaw University of Technology (Poland) and Ecole Centrale de Nantes (France).

My thesis was entitled "Whole-body online human motion imitation by a humanoid robot using task specificatrion". I did my research under the supervision of Prof. Sophie Sakka.
